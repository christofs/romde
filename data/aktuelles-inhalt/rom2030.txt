           Lecturer in French Studies University of Southampton - Modern Languages  Location:  Avenue Campus Salary:   £27,578 to £33,884 Full Time Permanent Closing Date:   Friday 03 August 2012 Interview Date:   To be confirmed Reference:  140412F4  The Department of Modern Languages at the University of Southampton is seeking to appoint a Lecturer in French/ Francophone Studies (Level 4), starting January 2013 or as soon as possible thereafter.
