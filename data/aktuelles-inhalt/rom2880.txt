Santander Post-Doctoral Senior Studentship Portuguese Literary and Cultural Studies
           Santander Post-Doctoral Senior Studentship Portuguese Literary and Cultural Studies Clare College Cambridge  Applications are invited for the above post, tenable at Clare College, Cambridge.   The postholder will be expected to engage in research and teaching activities of the Department.  The postholder will be expected to engage in research at post-doctoral level, and to contribute to teaching up to six hours a week within the Department during Full Term.  This may include lectures, seminars, classes and supervision of undergraduate students.  The candidate will be expected to hold a PhD.  They will have a strong profile, both in terms of research and publications, and with the promise to bring an outstanding input to the Department of Spanish and Portuguese.  They will be expected to have a native or excellent non-native command of Portuguese and English.   The pensionable stipend for this post is Â£22,608 fixed for three years.
