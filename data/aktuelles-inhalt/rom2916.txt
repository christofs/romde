Senior Research Fellowships: 'Literature in European Languages including English (after 500 AD)'
           Senior Research Fellowships University of Oxford - All Souls College, Oxford All Souls College proposes to elect three Senior Research Fellows with effect from 1 October 2012 (or an agreed later date): in 'History (after 500 AD)', 'Literature in European Languages including English (after 500 AD)', and 'Philosophy (including Ancient Philosophy)', (all subjects broadly conceived).  A Senior Research Fellowship is of comparable academic standing to a Professorship in the University of Oxford. Applicants are expected to have a correspondingly distinguished record of achievement in research.  For further particulars see the College's website: www.all-souls.ox.ac.uk. The deadline for applications will be Friday, 23 September 2011.  All Souls College is an equal opportunities employer.      Hartmut Duppel
