Lecturer in Spanish (below the bar)
           Lecturer in Spanish (below the bar) University College Dublin - UCD School of Languages and Literatures Ref: 005300  UCD College of Arts and Celtic Studies Specified purpose appointment covering extended leave Estimated duration: 1 yr. or less, if current incumbent returns from leave  The UCD School of Languages and Literatures wishes to make a temporary appointment to the post of Lecturer in Spanish, preferably with expertise in Latin American studies.  The successful candidate will prepare, design, deliver and assess high-quality language and literature modules in the Spanish undergraduate and graduate programmes, and will undertake administrative duties to be assigned by the Head of School or his nominee.
