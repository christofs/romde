Publishing editor/co-ordinator, Œuvres complètes de Voltaire
           The Complete Works of Voltaire / Œuvres complètes de Voltaire  Publishing editor/co-ordinator (3-year fixed-term contract, 1 June 2012–31 May 2015) Academic-related staff grade 6S: Salary range from £25,854 to £30,870 p.a. according to experience  The Complete Works of Voltaire/Œuvres complètes de Voltaire is the first ever critical edition of Voltaire’s complete writings in French, which will number over 200 volumes on completion in 2018 and is widely acclaimed as the definitive edition which will last for posterity.  We are seeking someone who is totally fluent in both spoken and written English and French and with at least two years editorial experience in academic publishing. You will work closely with academic editors and take responsibility for various stages of the tracking, delivery, copyediting and publication of individual texts (and volumes).  If you have the required language skills and publishing experience, like a scholarly challenge and would enjoy being fully involved in one of the most prestigious research projects in French literature currently being undertaken in Britain, then please click on the attached job description [link above].  Applications with full CV by e-mail (preferred) as soon as possible to Clare Fletcher (clare.fletcher@voltaire.ox.ac.uk). By post to Clare Fletcher, Voltaire Foundation, 99 Banbury Road, Oxford OX2 6JX. Closing date for applications is 30 May 2012. Interviews in June.
