Teaching Fellow (French)
           University of Leicester - School of Modern Languages  The Department of French in the School of Modern Languages seeks a fixed-term, full-time Language Teaching Fellow from January 2011. The role includes teaching at all levels in the field of French language and cultural studies (literature, cinema/visual arts), preparation of materials, marking and assessment.  The ability both to work autonomously and to act as team leader is essential, as is native-level competence in written and spoken French. Familiarity with using technology in the classroom, as well as experience at an appropriate level or in Higher Education, would be an advantage.
