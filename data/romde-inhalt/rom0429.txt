Assistant Professor in Brazilian Studies
           Applications are invited for an assistant professorship in Brazilian Studies with a special focus on contemporary Brazilian society. The position is affiliated with the BA and MA study programmes of Brazilian Studies and involves research, teaching and supervision duties in this area.  The position as assistant professor is a three-year fixed-term appointment, with the possibility of re-appointment to the tenured position of associate professor (lektor). The appointment begins on 1 August 2014 or as soon as possible thereafter.
