Digital Humanities is the annual international conference of the Alliance of Digital Humanities Organizations (ADHO). ADHO is an umbrella organization whose goals are to promote and support digital research and teaching across arts and humanities disciplines, drawing together humanists engaged in digital and computer-assisted research, teaching, creation, dissemination, and beyond, in all areas reflected by its diverse membership. The Digital Humanities conference in 2012 will be hosted by the University of Hamburg. The theme of the 2012 conference is ‘Digital Diversity: Cultures, languages and methods’.
