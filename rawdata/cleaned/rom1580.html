Fellowships ",Interdisciplinary humanities and culture studies",, IFK Internationales Forschungszentrum Kulturwissenschaften, Vienna, Austria Deadline: 1 July 2011 Website: www.ifk.ac.at/index.php/actual-calls.html Senior Fellowships are designed to support excellent scholars, who are well advanced in their academic careers. Qualified scholars are eligible without regard to nationality, institutional, or departmental affiliation, although preference is given to applicants of international repute who have published significant papers in recognized refereed journals and books that establish the author as a leading expert in his/her academic field. Research Fellowships are designed to support post-doctoral scholars early in their careers. Applicants must have a Doctorate or a Ph.D. at the time of application. Qualified scholars are eligible without regard to nationality, institutional, or departmental affiliation, although preference is given to qualified Austrian applicants. Areas of Special Interest: Most of the fellowships are unrestricted. Several, however, are desig-nated for applications related to the two IFK research foci – “Cultures of Evidence” and “Certain-ty Undermined – Life-worlds and Knowledge in Transition”.
