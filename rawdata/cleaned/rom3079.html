Teaching Fellow in Medieval and Renaissance Studies University College London - School of European Languages, Culture and Society Part Time : 18.25 hours per week (50% FTE) The appointment will be on UCL Grade 7. The salary range will be £35,557 - £38,594 per annum, inclusive of London Allowance. Salary pro-rata for part time vacancies Duties and Responsibilities The Italian Department, part of UCL's School of European Languages, Culture and Society, is the original home of Italian Studies in the UK. The successful candidate will contribute to teaching and examining in the area of medieval and Renaissance studies and at least one other area of Italian Studies at BA and MA level. The postholder will also be required to undertake administrative duties in the Department, to participate in extra-curricular seminars and other Departmental events and otherwise contribute to the intellectual life of the Department. The post is funded for two years in the first instance and the successful candidate is expected to start in September 2011. Key Requirements Applications are invited from candidates who have a PhD in Renaissance Studies and a strong publication record in the area of medieval and Renaissance Studies. Experience of teaching at undergraduate and graduate level is essential, as is near-native competency in Italian and English. Further Details For further details about the vacancy and how to apply on line please go to www.ucl.ac.uk/hr/jobs/ and search on Reference Number 1183065. Please also note that in addition to your CV, you should attach a cover letter outlining your interest and relevant experience for this post and one appropriate publication.
