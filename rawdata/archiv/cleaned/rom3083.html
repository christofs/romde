Teaching Fellow in Spanish University College London - European Languages Part Time : 12.05 hours per week (33% FTE) The appointment will be on UCL Grade 7. The salary range will be £35,707 - £38,744 per annum, inclusive of London Allowance. The Department of Spanish and Latin American Studies, part of UCL's School of European Languages, wishes to recruit a Teaching Fellow in Spanish. The successful candidate will contribute to Spanish language teaching and courses on Latin American literature. This is a fixed term appointment to cover sabbatical leave and will be available from approximately mid-September 2012 to mid-June 2013. The successful candidate will have expert knowledge in modern Spanish and Latin American studies and will have a PhD in this area. Native-speaker competence in English and near-native speaker competence in Spanish are essential, as is teaching experience at undergraduate or graduate level, preferably in a UK higher education institution.
