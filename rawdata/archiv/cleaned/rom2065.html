Lecturer in Spanish - 0.5 fte University of Portsmouth - School of Languages and Area Studies, Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences Fixed term contract for 2 years Salary: £15,898 - £17,373 Reference: 10002399 Closing date: 1st July 2011 The School wishes to appoint a 0.5 fte fixed term Lecturer to contribute to the teaching of a range of specialist and options units in Spanish, both at undergraduate degree level and on the Institution-Wide Languages Programme (IWLP). You will be expected to be native or near-native speakers of Spanish and have proven experience of teaching Spanish to adult learners, preferably at University level. Experience of teaching Hispanic Area Studies is desirable. You should have a relevant first degree and preferably be a qualified language teacher. A relevant MA or PhD is desirable, the candidate will ideally be research and/or knowledge transfer-active, and will be expected to contribute to the School and/or CEISR's profile in these areas.
