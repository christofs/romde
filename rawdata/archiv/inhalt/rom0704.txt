CfP: Almanaque de Estudos Latino Americanos
           CFP: Almanaque de Estudos Latino Americanos  Almanaque de Estudos Latino Americanos is an online journal published yearly by the Department of Art and Cultural Studies of the Universidade Federal Fluminense. [http://www.puro.uff.br/almanaque]  We are currently accepting articles in the following areas: music, literature, indigenous cultures, visual arts, dance, cinema and diaspora studies. We also welcome visual works of art, videos, fiction and poetry. The journal publishes in Portuguese, Spanish, English and French.
