Temporary Lectureship in Latin American Cultural Studies
           Temporary Lectureship in Latin American Cultural Studies University of Cambridge  cSalary: £36,862 - £39,107pa  Limit of tenure: until 31 December 2012  Applications are invited for a temporary Lectureship in Latin American Cultural Studies. The tenure of the Lectureship will be from 1 October 2011 until 31 December 2012 (15 months). The post would provide an excellent opportunity for an early-career researcher wishing to gain experience in teaching at undergraduate and postgraduate levels. The successful applicant will be expected to contribute to the teaching programme of the MPhil in Latin American Studies and also to give a modest number of lectures on relevant undergraduate papers on Latin American Culture for the Department of Spanish and Portuguese.  Applicants must be of a post-doctoral status, or must have submitted their PhD thesis by the interview date in order to be eligible to apply. Further particulars and application forms for the post can be downloaded from the website of the Centre of Latin American Studies [ www.latin-american.cam.ac.uk/lectureship.html ] or obtained from the Centre's Administrator, Mr Sam Mather (17 Mill Lane, Cambridge CB2 1RX, email sjrm2@cam.ac.uk), to whom completed applications should be sent.
