French Language Tutor (Part-time 2 Posts) University of York - Department of Language and Linguistic Science Ref: UoY00847 Two part-time posts are available for the academic year 2010-11. As members of a small team of French teaching staff you will take a key role in the teaching and examination of French language modules and related cultural studies on the degree course. You will contribute to the administration of the French degree programme and will participate in the development of teaching materials in French. You will be a native speaker of French and have an Honours degree (or equivalent). You will also have language teaching experience with adults. Language teaching experience in higher education would be desirable. Salary will be at the tutor rate of £26.75 per hour. For the first post up to three hours per week of teaching, for the second post up to four hours per week of teaching, over twenty weeks (from October 2010 to June 2011). Please note that the Department is in the process of expanding its language provision and there may be other opportunities for part-time teaching in the future. Closing date: Friday 16 July 2010 For further information and to apply on-line, please visit our website: www.york.ac.uk/jobs/ Alternatively contact HR Services on 01904 434835 quoting reference number UoY00847. The University of York is committed to diversity and has policies and developmental programmes in place to promote equality of opportunity.
