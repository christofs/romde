Applications are invited for the position of Fixed Term Lecturer in Translation Studies with Spanish in the Department of Languages, Translation and Media, College of Arts and Humanities, reference 5908. It is expected that the successful candidate will commence in post, no later than 3rd October 2011. This post requires a contribution to teaching and graduate supervision across the College and may include the development and delivery of new modules, in addition to sharing organisational and academic administrative responsibilities. This post provides a role for a candidate with research and academic potential and interest in Translation Studies (Spanish) research, to develop their career. The appointee will be expected to expand their own research activities and be capable of linking with existing research areas and research teams in the College. They will also be encouraged to develop fruitful interactions and collaborations with industry and others. The appointee will seek to operate at an international level and publish research in the major journals. Applicants will hold, or be on the point of obtaining, a higher degree in Translation Studies or Linguistics and will have evidence of research work for publications which will contribute to the College, or demonstrate the potential to achieve this standard of research. In addition applicants will have high levels of written, oral and interpersonal skills and demonstrate evidence of developing research objectives and proposals for own or joint research. Appropriate knowledge and skills to enable the effective preparation, co-ordination, delivery, and assessment of modules is also essential.
