CFP: Almanaque de Estudos Latino Americanos Almanaque de Estudos Latino Americanos is an online journal published yearly by the Department of Art and Cultural Studies of the Universidade Federal Fluminense. [http://www.puro.uff.br/almanaque] We are currently accepting articles in the following areas: music, literature, indigenous cultures, visual arts, dance, cinema and diaspora studies. We also welcome visual works of art, videos, fiction and poetry. The journal publishes in Portuguese, Spanish, English and French. Abstracts (250 words) should be sent by October 15 to: Natália Azevedo (assistant to the editor) Almanaque_las@vm.uff.br
