Part Time Language Teacher (Italian) London School of Economics and Political Science - Language Centre Salary: Band 5: £26,580 - £30,767 pa inclusive with potential to progress to £33,102 per annum inclusive (Pro-rata 0.1171). Status: Teaching Italian 4 hours per week during 20 weeks. The LSE Language Centre provides undergraduate degree options for nearly 200 students, an institution wide language programme for over 1,800 students, English for Academic Purposes for the whole School, a wide range of English foundation and pre-sessional programmes, and has also developed a range of tailored courses for business clients and governmental organisations. We are recruiting an enthusiastic Italian language teachers with a proven track record in teaching Italian (A1 to C2 Common European Framework of Reference) to international students. You should be educated to graduate level or equivalent and taught Italian from absolute beginners to advanced levels. An interest in social, political and economic issues as well as experience of using web based resources are also highly desirable. In addition to a good salary the benefits that come with this job include a defined benefits pension scheme, generous annual leave and excellent training and development opportunities.
