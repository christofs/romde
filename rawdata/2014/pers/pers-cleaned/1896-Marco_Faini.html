Research interests and projects:

I am currently working for the Edizione Nazionale delle Opere di Pietro Aretino (Roma, Salerno Editrice); I am planning to publish the critical Edition of the volume Poesie varie (2013).

At present I am writing a book on biblical-religious Epic in Italy in the 16th and 17th centuries (tentative title: «Heroic Martyrdom Unsung». The tradition of Christian Epic in Renaissance Italy and the European context).

Critical edition of Anton Francesco Doni’s imprese manuscripts.

Fields of interest:
Philological and Literary themes of the Modern Age in Italy, with special reference to Teofilo Folengo, Pietro Aretino, Torquato Tasso.
“Imprese” and emblem literature in XVI-XVII-century Europe.
Political, Religious and Philosophical problems of the early-Modern Age, with special reference to those figures who contributed to define European identity: the courtier, the knight, the secretary, the military commander.
Gabriele D’Annunzio and late XIX-early XX centuries novel.
