Teaching Fellow Spanish Language University of Leicester - School of Modern Languages Ref no: AHL00132 location: Leicester Job Purpose: At Leicester we’re going places. Ranked in the top 12 universities in Britain our aim is to climb further. A commitment to high quality fused with an inclusive academic culture is our hallmark and led the Times Higher Education to describe us as “elite without being elitist”. You will have native-level speaking ability in Spanish with knowledge of the British University system and a degree in arts or humanities. You will play a leading role in the organisation and delivery of Spanish language at all levels of undergraduate study. You must demonstrate a commitment to modern communicative approaches to language teaching and a willingness to develop resources to support teaching and learning. Preference will be given to candidates with a postgraduate teaching qualification and considerable recent experience of teaching Spanish Language in a UK institution of Higher Education. This is a fixed term contract for 9 months, starting in September 2011 The closing date for this post is midnight on Friday 29th July 2011. We anticipate that interviews will take place in week beginning 22nd August.
