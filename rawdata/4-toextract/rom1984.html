Language Tutor in French The University of Manchester Closing date: 31/10/2011 Reference: HUM-00108 Faculty / Organisational unit: Humanities Salary: £24,370 to £28,251 Employment type: Fixed Term Duration: 8 months in the first instance Hours per week: 35 Location: Oxford Road This post is tenable from 31 October 2011 for eight months in the first instance. The School is one of the largest and most successful such groupings in the UK, enjoying outstanding RAE scores, strong external grant income and an excellent record of postgraduate and undergraduate recruitment. It is located in the Faculty of Humanities and brings together all language-based activity of the University of Manchester. The University Language Centre is a constituent part of the School and delivers a distinct portfolio of courses. You will provide instruction in spoken and written French at all levels, contribute to course design, review and curriculum development, contribute to assessment at all levels, and work as part of a team led by the Programme Director for French. You will liaise with the Head of French Studies and the Programme Director for French Studies with regard to strategic approaches to the delivery of the French language curriculum. Informal enquiries Informal enquiries can be made to Dr Daron Burrows, Head of French Studies: Email: daron.burrows@manchester.ac.uk. The University of Manchester values a diverse workforce and welcomes applications from all sections of the community.

Daron Burrows, 30.10.2011
