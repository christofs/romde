Lectureship: French University College Cork (three year appointment) Applications are invited for a Lectureship in French (three-year appointment) in University College Cork. French is a leading discipline in teaching and research in the University, offering programmes at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels in a wide range of areas, including degrees in Arts, Business and Law. The successful applicant will hold or be near completion of a Ph.D. or equivalent qualification in French, and will contribute to teaching across the range of programmes in French, to research and to administration in these and other areas. Applications are sought in any area of culture or society in modern France, preference may be given to candidates with expertise in French society and politics, or cinema, or French and francophone cultural studies. Informal enquiries may be made to Professor Patrick O'Donovan by email: ptod@ucc.ie Salary Scale (new entrants 2011)*: €31,821 - €51,270 Existing public servants continuously employed in the public sector on/after 31st December 2010 will be placed on the 2010 salary scales (non new entrants). Closing date: 12 noon, Monday 22nd August 2011 Application forms must be completed and are available, together with full details, from hr.ucc.ie/employment.php or Department of Human Resources, University College, Cork, Ireland. Email: recruitment@per.ucc.ie / Tel: +353 21 4903603 / Fax: +353 21 4271568 Please note that an appointment to posts advertised will be dependent upon University approval, together with the terms of the employment control framework for the higher education sector
