Society for French Studies 52nd Annual Conference Queen Mary, University of London, 4 – 6 July 2011 The deadline for registering for the Annual Conference of the Society for French Studies is Friday 20 May 2011. All those wishing to register for the conference should go to www.sfs.ac.uk/conference.htm where they will be able to download the appropriate registration form. Postgraduates, retired and unwaged colleagues benefit from a significantly reduced rate (and must complete the PG/Retired/Unwaged form). For programme information see www.sfs.ac.uk/conference.htm
