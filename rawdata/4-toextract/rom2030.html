Lecturer in French Studies University of Southampton - Modern Languages Location: Avenue Campus Salary: £27,578 to £33,884 Full Time Permanent Closing Date: Friday 03 August 2012 Interview Date: To be confirmed Reference: 140412F4 The Department of Modern Languages at the University of Southampton is seeking to appoint a Lecturer in French/ Francophone Studies (Level 4), starting January 2013 or as soon as possible thereafter. Preference will be given to applicants with expertise in contemporary literary/ cultural studies (1870 onwards). Applicants should be keen to work in a research intensive and interdisciplinary Modern Languages environment. They should hold a PhD on a relevant topic, and be engaged in publication of their research. They should have experience of teaching in Higher Education together with advanced fluency in French and English.
