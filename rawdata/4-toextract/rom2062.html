Lecturer in Spanish University College London - School of European Languages Full Time: The appointment will be on UCL Grade 7. The salary range will be £35,557 - £38,594 per annum, inclusive of London Allowance. The Department of Spanish and Latin American Studies, part of UCL's School of European Languages, Culture and Society, is seeking to recruit a lecturer whose focus, both in teaching and research, lies in Spanish Peninsular Studies. The successful candidate will contribute to the Department's and School's teaching, research and administration at undergraduate and postgraduate level as required. The appointment is available from 1st September 2012. Candidates should have near-native competency in Spanish and a relevant PhD or equivalent qualification. Teaching experience at undergraduate or postgraduate level is essential as is a strong publication record in the field. For further details about the vacancy and how to apply on line please go to www.ucl.ac.uk/hr/jobs/ and search on Reference Number 1231708.
