2010 Call For Applications Columbia University Italian Academy for Advanced Studies in America Fellowships 2010-11 Columbia's Italian Academy for Advanced Studies in America invites applications for projects related to all aspects of cultural memory, to research at the intersection of history, ethnography, and anthropology, to the relationship between contemporary and historical cultures, and to the histories of art, music, literature, and philosophy. Applications dealing with the scientific and technological aspects of culture and memory are also encouraged. Special consideration will be given to proposals in the neurosciences relevant to the Academy's ongoing project in Art and Neuroscience. Three Alexander Bodini Research Fellowships are also offered: in Developmental and Adolescent Psychiatry, in Culture and Religion, and in Global Development and Finance. Fellowships are open to both US and non-US citizens at the post-doctoral and faculty level. Applications are encouraged from countries beyond Europe. Fellows receive a stipend, full health benefits, travel allowance, and an office in the Academy. The deadline is Friday, December 3, 2010.
