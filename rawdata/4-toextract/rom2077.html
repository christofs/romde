Lecturer (Teaching-Focused) in Portuguese The University of Manchester - School of Languages, Linguistics and Cultures Closing date: 15/09/2011 Reference: HUM/12813 The School is one of the largest and most successful such groupings in the UK, enjoying outstanding RAE scores, strong external grant income and an excellent record of postgraduate and undergraduate recruitment. It brings together all language-based activity of the University of Manchester. The University Language Centre is also a constituent part of the School and delivers a distinct portfolio of courses. You will liaise with the Head of Spanish, Portuguese, and Latin American Studies with regard to strategic approaches to high-quality teaching and learning in the department, convene several culture content courses (focusing mostly on Brazil) and Portuguese language courses both at the Undergraduate and MA levels including the supervision of undergraduate dissertations and MA theses and contribute to course design, review and curriculum development. You will also act as Residence Abroad coordinator for Portuguese, take forward the coordination of Portuguese Studies according to established practice, and liaise with external funding bodies, especially the Instituto Camoes. The post is tenable from 1 February 2012 to 1 January 2013. Interviews will be held between 10 October and 20 October 2011. Salary £32,751 – £35,788 p.a.
