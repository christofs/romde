Part-time Evening Class Lecturers in French (Maternity Cover) Imperial College London - Department of Humanities,South Kensington campus 1 October 2011 - 31 March 2012 The Humanities Department offers an evening class programme to students, staff and visitors to the College throughout the academic year (from October to March). The evening language programme offers classes at Beginners to Advanced levels. Lecturers in French are required for October 2011. The posts involve planning and teaching classes which take place between 6.00pm and 8.00pm for 20 weeks during the Autumn and Spring Terms (40 hours in total). You will be a graduate, preferably with a postgraduate qualification in modern languages, linguistics, or related field and with experience of adult education teaching. The salary for the 40 hour course will be £1460.80.
