Senior Lecturer in French Canterbury Christ Church University Reference Number: SLF/ELS Background The Faculty has a full-time vacancy for a Senior Lecturer in French within the Department of English and Language Studies. The post holder will be responsible for directing the BA Combined Honours programme in French, overseeing the staffing and provision of modern foreign language teaching across the University, including the Department’s own Contemporary Language Studies programme, and teaching on the BA French programme. You will have experience of teaching French in a Higher Education context, have a proven track record in published research, and hold an MA or equivalent qualification in French. You will also have a PhD in a relevant subject area. Experience of programme direction at HE level would be an advantage.
